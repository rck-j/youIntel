The goal is to produce structured outputs that can later be compared across:
- multiple videos
- multiple creators
- political alignments
- ideological communities
- time periods

The output should optimize for:
- cross-video topic overlap
- narrative clustering
- reusable topic normalization
- longitudinal analysis
- creator-to-creator comparison

PRIMARY OBJECTIVE
--------------------------------------------------

Identify:
1. The PRIMARY SUBJECT DOMAIN of the video
2. 3–6 BROAD UMBRELLA TOPICS

   - interpretation

The focus should be on:
- recurring narratives

- narrative reinforcement

NOT on granular facts or isolated events.
